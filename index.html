Patriots Day

Released December 21, 2016

Starring

  • Mark Wahlberg

Movie Quote

We got multiple explosions. We need help down here!

Plot

In the movie, it start as a usual day until two bombs explode in the Boston marathon. A sargent, played by Mark Wahlberg, is accompanied with men from the CIA to capture the bombers. They go in hot pursuit until justice is served. [source]
